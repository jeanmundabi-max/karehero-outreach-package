Context

KareHero has built the most complete adult care benefit in the UK. KPMG. Deloitte. BT Group. Nationwide. The product works at FTSE 100 scale. The problem is the channel that finds the next 230 employers.

Funnel Diagnostic

Where the funnel leaks.

Five stages. Two are broken. One is missing entirely. Everything else works.

🟢

Product — Strong

FTSE 100 clients validated. £10.6M in care funding accessed by families. Full care journey: assessment, placement, funding advice, legal support. No competitor matches this depth. Nothing to fix here.

🔴

Awareness — Broken

KareHero does not appear on page one of Google for "eldercare employee benefit UK." 3,483 LinkedIn followers despite FTSE 100 clients. No published case studies. The HR Director who has not yet met Stephanie does not know KareHero exists.

🟡

Reach — Partial

Event and network-led reach works within existing circles. Does not scale to 230+ EfC network members. Cannot reach the HR Director at a company outside Stephanie's network who is evaluating benefits right now.

🟢

Commercial Process — Working

Matthew Johnson (CRO) is in place. The commercial process exists. Deals close at FTSE 100 scale. When KareHero gets into an evaluation, it wins. The product sells itself.

🔴

Top of Funnel — Missing

No systematic outbound channel reaching HR Directors in active benefits review windows. No mechanism to identify which companies in the 230+ EfC network are evaluating right now. Every deal requires a warm introduction or a lucky discovery. This is the primary gap.

The two red lights are in one place: awareness and top-of-funnel reach. Everything downstream — product, commercial, delivery — works. Install the outbound channel and the red lights turn green.

Force 1: Carer's Leave Act Anniversary

Force 2: Pre-Series A Pressure

Stephanie is in investor conversations. Every large employer signed before the Series A becomes a case study in the fundraising deck. Investors want repeatable commercial traction, not just named logos. The window to build that evidence is the 90 days before the raise.

Force 3: Q3 Benefits Decision Cycle

UK employer benefit decisions peak in Q3 and Q4. New benefits approved in Q3 go live in Q4 or the following January. The companies in this list that are in their annual review cycle are making decisions in the next 60 to 90 days.
